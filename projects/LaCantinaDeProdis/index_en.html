About Application

LaCantinaDeProdis is an application aimed at managing the canteen of Prodis Terrassa (a non-profit social initiative terrace entity intended for assistance and the comprehensive promotion of adult people with intellectual disability, mental illness or cerebral palsy).

Among his functionality, we find: to make orders (either at the barn level, as in customer level), to be able to view the content of the orders and to be able to view the total price of the order.
