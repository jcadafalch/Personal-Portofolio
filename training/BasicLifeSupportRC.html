Basic Life Support

  • Date: 14/07/2021 - 15/07/2021
  • Website: Link

Learning the most efficient techniques to deal with a medical emergency until health personnel arrive.

These trainings follow the new guidelines on first aid published by the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) and by the 2015 Guidelines of the European Ressuscitation Council (ERC)


Objectives

- General

Provide participants with the necessary knowledge to give an initial and efficient response that allows to maintain the victim in the best possible conditions until he is placed in the hands of sanitary teams.

- Specific
  • Know and know how to apply the protocols of action.
  • Know how to identify and resolve a vital emergency situation.
  • Know the prevention measures.
  • Know how to apply Basic Life Support protocols.
